Mintorie
E-Commerce · Indian Handicrafts
India's best handicrafts — great prices, authentic quality.

Mintorie is a curated e-commerce marketplace celebrating India's rich artisan heritage. We connect skilled craftspeople with customers who value authenticity, offering genuine Made-in-India handicrafts with free shipping and competitive pricing.

  • Authentic handcrafted Indian goods

PastePort
Developer Tool · Clipboard Sync
Copy here. Paste there.

PastePort is a cross-device clipboard-sharing app built for developers, designers, and power users. Transfer text, links, images, and files instantly between Android, Chrome, and terminal — encrypted end-to-end, no cables required.

  • AES-256-GCM end-to-end encryption
  • Android, Chrome & CLI support
  • Real-time delivery across devices
  • Offline queuing & local history
